GUIDED TOURS

Welcome explorers. Our guided tours will take you into an amazing experience.

Jeep Tours

Get on board into the Jeep tours. The driver will take you in the mountains between Durango and Silverton. Jeep tour will show you a numerous variety of wildflowers, rare plants and wildlife. Impressive waterfalls and geological features around the high-alpine meadows.

We will follow the La Plata River to La Plata City. There will find an old mining town established in 1875. Afterwards, we will continue our trip to the peak of Kennebec Pass (12,000 ft above sea level). ewIn our way back we will se Cortez and Telluride from the top of the pass.

Kids are extra entertained with our “Pan For Gold” scavenger hunt. Find the items on the checklist and get some fool’s gold!

*The roads are not well maintained and not often used which results in a bumpy, bouncy tour! Bring sun cover, suncream, a reusable water bottle, snacks, and your camera!
