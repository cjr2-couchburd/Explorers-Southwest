GUIDED TOURS

Welcome explorers. Our guided tours will take you into an amazing experience.

Jeep Tours

Adventure Level - Mild
Location - Durango. In the Mountains between Durango and Silverton
Family Friendly- Yes
Season- May to Early October
Numbers- Single up to Groups of 8 people

Prices
1/2 Day - $60pp / Youth (5-16) $25pp / Under 5s Free

Half day Trips:
Meet at 8:00am for a 9:00am start.
OR 1oom for a 2pm start.

Half day- 9am trip: return by 12pm. / 2pm trip: return by 5pm

Basic Information about the Jeep Tour:
Our guided Jeep Tour will show you:
● numerous wildflowers and rare plants
● a large variety wildlife
● high-alpine meadows
● avalanche gullies
● Impressive waterfalls and geological features

Follow the La Plata River to La Plata City, and old mining town established in 1875. Continue to the peak of Kennebec Pass at 12,000ft above sea level

See all the way to Cortez and Telluride from the top of the pass.

Kids are extra entertained with our “Pan For Gold” scavenger hunt. Find the items on the checklist and get some fool’s gold!

*The roads are not well maintained and not often used which results in a bumpy, bouncy tour! Bring sun cover, suncream, a reusable water bottle, snacks, and your camera!

Day Trips to Mesa Verde National Park

Adventure Level - Mild to Moderate
Location - Meet in Durango for a 30 minutes drive to Mesa Verde
Family Friendly- Yes
Season- March to October
Numbers- Up to 10 Guests

Prices
Full Day - $175pp / Youth (5-16) $95pp / under 5 $10.00

Full day - includes a robust packed lunch from the local deli (vegetarian / vegan options available upon request). Return by 5pm. Bring sturdy walking shoes, sun cover, suncream, a reusable water bottle and your camera!

Mesa Verde is one of only 20 World Heritage Sites in North America

● Meet the Explorers Southwest Main Office at 8:00am for an 8:30am start.
● Relax in our fully air conditioned tour bus while we take a 45 minute drive to Mesa Verde National Park on old Spanish Trail Trade Routes.
● We will then go straight to the Mesa Verde visitor center just inside the park for an introductory park orientation.
● Enjoy breathtaking views of mountains ranges from 4 states (Colorado, Arizona, Utah, and New Mexico) on the way to Far View Ancestral Village.
● Explore Cliff Palace, the largest and most famous cliff dwelling in North America
● Kids can earn their “Junior Park Ranger Badge” while interacting with one of Mesa Verde’s park rangers.
● Enjoy a robust lunch provided by a local deli in Durango.
● Explore the Mesa Loop and take in a variety of archeological sites, cliff dwellings, pit house and scenic overlooks.
● Return to Durango: 4-5pm
